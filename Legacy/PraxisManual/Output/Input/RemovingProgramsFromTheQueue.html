Removing programs from the queue

  1. Switch to Praxis Job page and select one or more Jobs or Job-parts.
  2. Right click and use the Reset Job/Reset Part panel command to revoke the job parts from the production queue.
  3. The RA program list is automatically updated with the updated queue.
